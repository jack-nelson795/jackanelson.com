          <ul>
            <li>Developed an ML surrogate model using Bayesian Optimization, Gaussian Process Regression, and Latin Hypercube Sampling.</li>
            <li>Designed to iterate upon and optimize JAXA’s M12-02 hypersonic test bench.</li>
            <li>Built a fully automated, headless optimization pipeline that generates scramjet geometries in FreeCAD, simulates them in SU2, and trains a surrogate model to predict flow outcomes.</li>
            <li>The model iteratively learns by comparing predictions to CFD results, enabling rapid design space exploration by replacing costly simulations with learned approximations and evaluating only top-performing designs per generation.</li>
          </ul>
